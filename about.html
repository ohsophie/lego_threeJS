In this web-game you can build a tower from Lego bricks.
Use the buttons to create a new LEGO brick and continue building your LEGO tower.
Configure the light and camera z-index (also accessible by twisting the mouse wheel) in controls when needed.
Move the camera around by pressing right mouse button.
Rotate the camera by pressing left mouse button.
Delete bricks by clicking a "cross" button.

This project is created as part of the course "Computer graphics basics" in KPI TUKE.
